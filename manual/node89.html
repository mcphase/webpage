Landé Factor

In order to calculate the magnetisation and Zeeman term, the Landé factor has to be entered in the single ion property file. For a rare earth ion this factor gives the ratio of negative angular momentum / magnetic moment. Note, for intermediate coupling schemes this factor has no meaning (should be set to zero). 

#Lande Factor
GJ=0.85714
