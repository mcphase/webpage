Using the so1ion module in McPhase and McDisp

The use of the so1ion module in mcphas is necessary in order to go beyond the capabilities of singleion (for instance the calculation of magnetisation or specific heat or if the exchange interaction given in equations (1) and (3) shall be taken into account).
