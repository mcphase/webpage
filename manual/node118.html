External module function dpel1 - used by mcdisp

In order to calculate neutron intensities for RAMAN scattering the external single ion module has to provide the transition matrix elements of the electric dipole moment in units of pm. The

The format to be used is:

char ** sipffilename,ComplexVector & p1,float & maxE, ComplexMatrix & est)
